Markdown
# Required NCR Checklist

☐ Requirement clearly referenced (drawing/spec)
☐ Actual value or observed defect recorded (quantified)
☐ Gap between required and actual defined
☐ Evidence attached or referenced
☐ Severity correctly classified
☐ Affected quantity and population at risk identified
☐ Material segregated and identified
☐ Disposition defined and approved
☐ Containment actions documented (if applicable)
