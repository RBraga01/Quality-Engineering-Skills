❗ 1. Falta “Required Audit Checklist” no topo
👉 esta é a melhoria com maior impacto.
✅ Sugestão HIGH IMPACT
Markdown
# Required Internal Audit Checklist

☐ Define audit scope, criteria, and objectives
☐ Verify auditor competence and independence
☐ Audit the process using open questions and objective evidence
☐ Classify findings correctly (Major / Minor / OFI)
☐ Record evidence clearly and traceably
☐ Check effectiveness of previous corrective actions
☐ Confirm process performance and KPIs
☐ Issue a balanced audit report (findings + strengths)
☐ Track corrective actions to closure
``
Show more lines

❗ 3. Falta secção de amostragem
Isto é importante em auditoria real.
✅ Sugestão
Markdown
Use sampling: interview multiple people, review multiple records, and verify actual practice at the workplace.
One record is not enough to confirm systemic conformity.
Show more lines

❗ 4. Falta governance do follow-up

Markdown

❗ 5. Falta orientação sobre redação de findings
Tens o report format, mas falta uma regra muito útil.
✅ Sugestão
Markdown
Each finding should include:
- Requirement
- Objective evidence
- Statement of non-conformity
Avoid conclusions without linking evidence to the specific requirement.
