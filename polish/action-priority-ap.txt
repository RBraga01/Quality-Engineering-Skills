❗ 1. Falta “Required Execution Checklist”
Tal como nos outros, isto melhoraria muito a consistência.
✅ Sugestão HIGH IMPACT
Markdown
# Required AP Review Checklist

☐ Confirm the linked Failure Effect, Mode, and Cause are correct
☐ Confirm S is based on the Failure Effect
☐ Confirm O is based on the Failure Cause with current prevention controls
☐ Confirm D is based on current detection controls
☐ Assign AP using the full AIAG-VDA AP table
☐ For every H-AP item, define action or documented escalation
☐ Reassess AP only after action implementation
☐ Verify Special Characteristics are rated S = 9 or 10
