# Required IATF Audit Checklist

☐ Verify all three audit streams are planned and executed
☐ Confirm CSR register is current and implemented
☐ Check contingency plans are documented, reviewed, and tested
☐ Verify special characteristics are consistent across all documents
☐ Confirm supplier monitoring and escalation are active
☐ Check Control Plan ↔ PFMEA linkage
☐ Verify traceability and suspect material isolation capability
☐ Review temporary deviations for approval and expiry control
☐ Verify problem-solving methodology is applied with VOE
☐ Confirm management review includes IATF supplemental inputs
